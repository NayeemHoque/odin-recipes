Creamy Mushroom Pasta

A tasty pasta recipe that is rich and delicious, making for a wholesome meal!

Ingredients

  • Mushrooms
  • Pasta (of your liking)
  • heavy cream
  • Garlic
  • black pepper powdered
  • Salt
  • onions
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Heat up a bit of olive oil on a frying pan in low heat
  2. Chop an onion and add the onion into the oil
  3. while the onion caramelises, stiry occassionally to make sure it doesn't burn
  4. slice the mushrooms into flat thin pieces
  5. When the onion turns brown, add the mushrooms, stir and let it simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Dice a clove of garlic into fine pieces
  7. Add salt, the diced garlic and black pepper once the mushrooms change colour
  8. Boil hot water in a large pan
  9. After bringing the water to boiling point, add salt and then your pasta
  10. Add heavy cooking cream to the mushrooms and stir
  11. After the pasta is coming close to al dente, transfer it from the large pan to the mushrooms
  12. Add 200ml of pasta water to the mushrooms and stir well
  13. Now cook the pasta for an additional three to four minutes and take it off the heat
  14. Add chopped parsley as garnish and serve!
