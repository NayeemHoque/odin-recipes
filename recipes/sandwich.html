Sandwiches

A perfectly nourishing and savoury mael to easily make at home if you have a sandwich maker!

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Tuna can
  • Carrots
  • (Optionl)Green Chillis

Steps

  1. Put two spoons of mayonnaise into a bowl
  2. Take out the tuna from the can and mix it with the mayonnaise
  3. Finely dice the carrots and mix into the mayonnaise and tuna
  4. Add pepper and salt to liking
  5. Cut the bread slices into triangles, and start putting the mix as a filling between two triangles
  6. Now put the bread into a sandwich maker and Press
  7. Wait till you see slightly brown lines to take them out
  8. And there you have it! Enjoy a warm evening snack at the convenience of your home.
