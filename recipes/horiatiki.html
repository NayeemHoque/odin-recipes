Horiatiki Recipe

A perfectly healthy salad to beat the heat of the summer with its burst of flavours that leaves you fulfilled!


Ingredients

  • Tomatoes (red, ripe and firm tomatoes are recommended)
  • Cucumber
  • Red onions
  • Olives (best if Kalamata olives are available)
  • Capsicum
  • Feta Cheese
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Salt and drieg oregano

Steps

  1. Slice cucumbers into half moons
  2. cut the capsicum into small thin strips
  3. Chop the tomatoes into small pieces
  4. Thinly slice the onions into half rings
  5. Now put them all into a bowl, add the olives
  6. Add the feta cheese
  7. add the seasonings (salt, organo), and the dressings (olive oil, vinegar)
  8. Now mix all the ingredients and press on the feta cheese so it breaks down and spreads to the salad
  9. And there you have it! Ready to serve
